Audi A3 Effortlessly Combines Driving and Safety
There is nothing more important than the safety of you and your loved ones on the road,
which is why you need a vehicle that takes safety as seriously as you do. This is exactly
what you will get with the Audi A3 thanks to its vast collection of impressive safety features.

Providing Stability When It Is Needed
Whether the roads are a little bad or you just happen to get a little too heavy-footed
with the gas pedal, sometimes the vehicle starts to lose traction and stability.
When this happens, the vehicle needs to have a method of bringing the ride back under
control so that you are always able to help keep the vehicle out of the ditch.
One of the ways in which the A3 is able to do this is through the use of the
Electronic Stability Control feature. This helpful system combines forces with the
vehicle's Anti-Lock Brake System in order to ensure that the vehicle maintains as much
stability as possible under any circumstances.

Sturdy and Lightweight Figure
When you find out that something is incredibly durable, the last thing that you
expect is for it to also be lightweight. However, that is exactly what you will find
with the intricately designed A3 metal frame. The lightweight materials are able to provide
impressive strength while also weighing far less than many other models in the class.
The reduced weight and increased durability form a mixture that helps to minimize the
damage that occurs in the event of a collision. The frame also utilizes strategically-placed
crumple zones, which also designed to help significantly reduce the impact of a collision
on you and your passengers.

Old Methods With a New Look
Improving safety on the road does not always have to feel like you are reinventing the wheel.
Sometimes, the tried and true methods just require a little bit of modification in order to produce
the best results. The perfect example of this is the airbag system which is used in the A3. With optimized
airbag positions throughout the vehicle, a superior safety belt quality, and strategically adjusted headrests,
the vehicle is able to deliver one of the safest environments on the road.
Get a vehicle that is as safe to drive as it is fun by purchasing the Audi A3 today.

A3 Lets You Customize Your Driving Experience
Not all drivers are the same, so why should all vehicle cockpits be the same? The Audi A3 takes this philosophy
and brings it to life by offering an unprecedented level of customization to make your driving experience exactly how
you want it each and every time.

Modes for Different Moods
By getting the available Audi virtual cockpit system, you enable yourself to create the driving experience that you desire.
The large full color display in the vehicle is capable of doing almost anything you ask of it.
For a more classic and traditional feel, you can set the screen up in Classic mode, which enlarges the speedometer and
tachometer to meet the style of many other models. However, if you are in the mood for something unique and futuristic,
then simply flip over to the infotainment mode. This will significantly reduce the size of the speedometer and talking meter
in order to provide a large panoramic view of either navigation or infotainment. No matter what you want to see you, it can
all be brought to your line of sight with ease.

Simple Design for Maximum Control
Having a bunch of buttons cluttering up your dashboard can not only look awful but also be incredibly confusing. The A3 is able
to get rid of this issue by instead going with a more minimalist design. A large part of this unique design is the available MMI®
rotary controller system.
This large and sleek looking dial integrates a touch screen pad in the middle of it, which allows you to use your fingers in order
to control the vehicle like never before. The system also comes with a function key conveniently located on either side of the rotary
controller, meaning that you can easily control a variety of tasks by programming the buttons to do certain things.
Do not change your driving habits to match your vehicle, change your vehicle to match your driving habits by purchasing
the highly customizable Audi A3 today.
